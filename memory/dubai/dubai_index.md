### SALIK TOLL GATE SYSTEM

**What is Salik**: Electronic toll collection on Dubai's major roads. No physical gates—automatic detection via windshield tag.

**How It Works**: 
- Salik tag on windshield (AED 100 deposit + AED 50 recharge)
- Automatic deduction when passing toll gate
- Multiple gates on Sheikh Zayed Road and major highways

#### DYNAMIC PRICING MODEL (Current as of Jan 31, 2026)

### SHEIKH ZAYED ROAD (E11): The Main Arterial

**Why This Matters**: Sheikh Zayed Road is Dubai's busiest highway. Almost every cross-city journey involves it. Understanding its conditions is understanding Dubai traffic.

**Sheikh Zayed Road Facts**:
- Runs north-south through entire Dubai (parallel to coast, slightly inland)
- 14 lanes wide at widest point
- Multiple Salik toll gates along route
- JLT is directly adjacent (east side of Cluster C borders E11)
- Carries 300,000+ vehicles daily

**Most Congested Sections from JLT**:

| Section | Distance from JLT | Peak Impact | Notes |
|---------|------------------|-------------|-------|
| **Trade Centre to Dubai Marina** | 10-15 km south | ⚠️ Extreme | Downtown corridor, worst congestion |
| **Al Safa Interchange** | 12 km south | ⚠️ Major chokepoint | Business Bay area, heavy throughout peak |
| **Business Bay to Al Quoz** | 14-18 km south | Medium-Heavy | Persistent slow-downs |
| **Jebel Ali Area** | 15 km south | Moderate | Secondary bottleneck going south |

**Alternative Routes** (when Sheikh Zayed is gridlocked):

1. **Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road (E311)**: 
   - Parallel route, ~8 km inland
   - Toll-free
   - Still heavy during inbound morning (Sharjah → Dubai commuters)
   - Good for north-south travel avoiding central Dubai

2. **Al Khail Road (E44)**:
   - Eastern alternate
   - Connects inner-emirate destinations
   - Heavy from Ras Al Khor to Dubai Hills during peak

3. **Emirates Road (E611)**:
   - Northern alternate to airport
   - Monitored for incidents
   - Heavy congestion in both directions during peak

**Real-Time Traffic Tools**:
- **RTA Dubai Drive** app: Live traffic conditions
- **Google Maps**: Real-time routing with incident alerts
- **Waze**: Community-reported traffic and police
- **S'hail Dubai** app: Public transport + traffic updates
